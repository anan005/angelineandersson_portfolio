Who am I?

Hi there! My name is Angeline, I'm a UX-designer student who just started my 4th and last semester at Fagskolen Kristiania in Oslo, Norway.

Currently searching for internship in Berlin, from Mars until end of April.

Long story short: I born and raised in Oslo. I love using my free time on outdoor activities, like hiking and camping, but as well appreciate a good movie or experimenting with food, while listening to good music.

Why UX-Design?

Could say I have found the passion for solving the most commom problem there is: Bad User Experiences.
There is a thin line between a good digital design and good interface. But one thing they have in common to make a good user experience; empathy for the user.

I've always enjoyed working with people, and I can still say I value the oppurtiny to do so. By using experience and relevant tools from my earlier studies, Intercultural Communication, I can more easily understand both the user and the target group.

That being addressed, i'm looking forward to use my skills I have gained during my studies working more with research, interaction design, user experience, information architecture and Web Accessibility. The keywords of making the experience for the user friendly, aesthetic and suitable for everybody in all kinds of situation.
