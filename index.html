Why UX-Design?

I have found a passion for solving the most complex problem: Bad User Experience. It's never an easy task to do so, but if you how to understand the users, by emphasizing with them, the ideation of the experience and product will have a bigger chance to meet their needs and behaviour.

I've always enjoyed helping people, and the opportunity to learn more about the diversity of problems we people go through daily. With my experience from earlier studies, intercultural communication, also helps me understand their cultural values.

In June I will finish my studies, and I'm already looking forward to learning more, through new experiences and work. And optimizing guidelines to produce a suitable product for everybody with all kinds of needs.
